Escape The Crime Scene - Team Project(Unity)
More Details

A short game where the player has to hide all the clues related to the crime and leave the scene, before the cops arrive.

Personal contributions:

  • UI: created the UI pop-ups and feedback for the game.
  • Level Design: determinating the layout of the map and placement of the interactables.
  • Testing: tested the map and fine tuned the times and placements in such a way it would not be to hard or easy for the player to complete the objective.
  • Prototyping Mechanics and UI: prototyped different mechanics linked to specific UI.
